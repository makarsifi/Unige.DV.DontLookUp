Climate change was first discovered in 1896

Early signs were: rising in world-wide temperature, increase in droughts and tropical storms becoming more severe

Drivers responsible for climate change are Energy Consumption, Transportation Emmissions, Lack of Waste Managment, Agriculture Fertilizers.
